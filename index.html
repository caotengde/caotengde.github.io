About

A compact record of research, projects and notes.

I am a Ph.D. student at hessian.AI and the Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Lab at Technische Universität Darmstadt. My research interests include deep generative models, probabilistic and geometric representation learning, 3D vision, neuro-symbolic learning and reinforcement learning.

This site keeps a compact record of my research projects, publications and notes. I write mostly about visual perception systems that can segment, track, reconstruct and reason about objects in dynamic scenes.

Research

Research Interests

01

3D Vision and Robotics

Object segmentation, 6D pose estimation, tracking, NOCS reconstruction and geometry-aware perception for robotic systems.

02

Deep Generative Models

Transformers, VAEs, diffusion models and flow matching for vision, representation learning and 3D reconstruction.

03

Neuro-Symbolic Learning

Reinforcement learning systems that acquire structured world knowledge and use it for interpretable decision-making.

Projects

Selected Projects

2025

STORM

Segment, Track, and Object Re-Localization from a Single Image. A unified framework for annotation-free robust 6D object pose estimation and tracking.

2025 - now

Memory-A1

A neuro-symbolic memory agent that builds and updates structured world knowledge from interaction trajectories for reinforcement learning.

2025

NOCSFlow-3D

A flow-matching framework for NOCS-based 6D pose estimation, object mask prediction and 3D reconstruction from a single RGB image.

2024 - 2025

Rope Trajectory Tracking

A conditional diffusion approach for rope trajectory tracking and intersection detection, evaluated with synthetic data generated in Unity3D.
